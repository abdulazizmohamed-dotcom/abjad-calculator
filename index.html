Abjad

Abjad is a classical letter–number system in which each Arabic letter carries a fixed numerical value. This page gives the full reference table and a worked example, and then you can open the calculator to compute any word instantly.

Worked example

Letter-by-letter

To calculate a word, you take each letter’s value and add them together.

الله

ا = 1
ل = 30
ل = 30
ه = 5

Total = 1 + 30 + 30 + 5 = 66

The calculator does the same summation automatically, and will warn you if it encounters characters outside the standard Abjad set.
